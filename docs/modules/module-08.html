Module 8

IR Angle: Retrieval, Ranking, and Summarization Bias

For IR, the key question is not only what the model says, but what it retrieves, ranks, summarizes, cites, omits, or overexposes.

Core Readings

  • FA*IR.
  • Fairness of Exposure in Rankings.
  • Fairness in Ranking: A Survey.
  • Auditing Search Engines for Differential Satisfaction Across Demographics.
  • Entman (1993) and Eberl et al. (2018).

Migration IR Questions

  • Are security/criminality frames overexposed?
  • Are migrant-led or local-language sources underexposed?
  • Do generated answers cite government sources more than legal aid or community sources?
  • Does summarization erase frame diversity?
