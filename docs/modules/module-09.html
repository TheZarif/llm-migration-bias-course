Multilingual and Cross-Cultural Issues

Migration discourse is locally framed. English-only prompts can erase local politics, legal categories, and community-specific terms.

Core Readings

  • CIVICS.
  • Global Voices, Local Biases.
  • Learning the Topic, Not the Language.
  • Perspectival Mirror of the Elephant.
  • HONEST and Data Statements for NLP.

Key Risks

  • English-centric migration categories.
  • Translation artifacts and loss of local legal meaning.
  • Uneven source coverage across languages.
  • Cultural value mismatch in prompts and annotations.
