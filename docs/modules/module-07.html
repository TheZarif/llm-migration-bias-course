Module 7

LLMs as Decision-Makers and Decision Aids

This is where representational harm can become allocative harm: who gets admitted, helped, believed, ranked, flagged, or denied.

Core Readings

  • Tamkin et al. (2023), discrimination in language-model decisions.
  • PapersPlease and Digital Gatekeepers.
  • The Silicon Ceiling.
  • Hofmann et al. (2024), dialect and covertly racist decisions.

Decision Settings

  • Visa approval and admissibility.
  • Asylum credibility and documentation assessment.
  • Emergency housing or aid eligibility.
  • Hiring, welfare, risk, or fraud triage.
