Module 11

Open Problems and Research Opportunities

The literature is rich, but migration-specific LLM and IR evaluation remains thin.

Research Gaps

  • Migration-specific LLM benchmarks remain underdeveloped.
  • IR-style exposure and source-diversity metrics are underused in LLM migration-bias work.
  • Legal status, asylum status, and nationality are often conflated.
  • Multilingual evaluation still lags behind English-centric work.
  • Threat, group position, dehumanization, and legal violence are rarely operationalized in LLM benchmarks.
