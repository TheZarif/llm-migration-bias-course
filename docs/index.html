LLM Bias, Migration, and Information Retrieval

A compact, theoretically grounded learning path for researchers entering work on social bias, migration, language models, and retrieval systems.

Start Module 1 View Bibliography

What This Course Does

Grounds LLM work in social theory

Bias is connected to stereotype content, threat, dehumanization, framing, deservingness, legal violence, and boundary-making.

Connects NLP benchmarks to migration

Classic benchmark designs become building blocks for migration-specific probes, not final answers.

Brings in the IR perspective

Retrieval, ranking, summarization, citation, exposure, and source authority are treated as central sites of harm.
